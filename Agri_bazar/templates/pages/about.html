About Us

At this stage of the developed world, our country is now largely dependent on agricultural work. But in our country where farmers are not properly evaluated, I think something should be done for farmers that can keep pace with the developed world and the developed world means we are the age of internet. Farmers will be able to sell their produce or crop directly to buyers through my e-commerce site and get fair price, At the same time consumers will be able to buy the pure produce directly from the farmers as per the demand.
